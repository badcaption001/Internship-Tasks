About Arjith Singh

Arjith Singh, born Jonny Vincent on March 6, 1987, in Chennai, India, is one of the most acclaimed playback singers in the Indian music industry. Known for his soulful voice and versatility, he has sung in multiple languages including Tamil, Telugu, Hindi, and Malayalam, captivating audiences with his emotive renditions.

Arjith inspires me with his dedication to music and his ability to convey deep emotions through song. His journey from a young talent to a global icon shows the power of passion and perseverance in the world of music.

Biography & Achievements

  • 1987: Born in Chennai, India.
  • 2007: Made his debut with the song "Putham Pudhu Kaalai" from the film Paruthiveeran.
  • 2010: Won his first Filmfare Award for Best Male Playback Singer for "En Kadhal Solla" from Aadhalal Kadhal Seiveer.
  • 2012: Achieved massive success with "Why This Kolaveri Di" from 3, which became a viral sensation.
  • 2014: Won multiple awards for his work in films like Madras Cafe and Kaththi.
  • 2019: Released his first independent album "Nerukku Ner."
  • Legacy: Over 100 film songs, multiple awards, and a fanbase spanning generations.
